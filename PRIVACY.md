# Privacy Policy

**GiftTracker - History/Planner**
Operated by Michael Turner
Last updated: June 13, 2026

---

## Overview

GiftTracker - History/Planner ("the App") is a mobile application that helps you track gifts you've given and plan future gifts for the people in your life. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly
- **Account information** — your email address and password when you create an account
- **People/contacts** — names, relationships, birthdays, photos, and notes you add manually or import from your device's contacts
- **Gift records** — gift names, occasions, dates, prices, notes, and product links you enter
- **Gift preferences** — interest tags and preference notes you add for people on your list

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data** — basic app usage information to help us improve the App
- **Device information** — device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes

### Information We Do Not Collect
- We do not collect your location
- We do not collect payment card details (payments are handled entirely by Apple's App Store)
- We do not access your contacts without your explicit permission
- We do not read your contacts automatically — contact import only occurs when you manually initiate it

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

- Provide and operate the App's core functionality
- Sync your data across your devices via secure cloud storage
- Send push notification reminders you have explicitly scheduled (Premium subscribers only)
- Respond to support requests

We do not use your personal information for advertising, and we do not sell your data to any third party.

---

## Amazon Affiliate Disclosure

The App includes an optional feature allowing you to save Amazon product links for planned gifts. When you save an Amazon link, the App automatically appends an Amazon Associates affiliate tracking tag to that link. This means if you make a purchase through a saved link, the App developer may earn a small commission from Amazon at no additional cost to you.

This is standard Amazon Associates program participation and is disclosed here in accordance with Amazon's program policies and applicable law.

---

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party cloud infrastructure provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Each user's data is isolated and protected using row-level security — meaning your data is never accessible to other users of the App.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

- **Supabase** (supabase.com) — secure cloud database and authentication
- **RevenueCat** (revenuecat.com) — subscription and in-app purchase management
- **Amazon Associates** (affiliate-program.amazon.com) — affiliate link program
- **Apple** (apple.com) — App Store, Apple Sign In, and push notification delivery

## Subscriptions

Premium subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Michael Turner does not collect or store your payment information. Subscription management, cancellation, and refunds are handled through your Apple ID account settings.

---

## Push Notifications

The App may request permission to send push notifications to remind you of planned gift dates (Premium feature). You can revoke notification permissions at any time through your device's Settings app. We only send notifications that you have explicitly scheduled within the App.

---

## Your Rights and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Upon request, we will delete your account data from our systems within 30 days.

You may also delete individual people, gifts, or other records directly within the App at any time.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
